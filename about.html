About the Website

Welcome to the Emotion-Based Song Recommendation platform! This website was created to help people discover music that resonates with their emotional state, offering a unique approach to music discovery. Whether you’re feeling happy, sad, angry, or neutral, our platform tailors song recommendations to match your current mood.

Through advanced emotion detection technology and powerful machine learning models, we provide personalized music suggestions to improve your emotional well-being. Whether you're looking for something to uplift your mood, embrace your sadness, or get energized, we’ve got the perfect track for every emotion.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to connect people with music that reflects their emotions and helps them feel better. We believe that music has the power to heal, energize, and inspire. By using state-of-the-art emotion recognition technology, we aim to make the process of finding the right music effortless and enjoyable.

How It Works

Once you access the website, the emotion detection system uses facial recognition to analyze your expressions through your webcam. Whether you’re smiling, frowning, or displaying any other emotional expression, the system instantly detects your emotion and recommends a playlist based on it.
