How It Works

At the core of our platform lies cutting-edge emotion detection technology, which powers personalized song recommendations tailored to your current emotional state. By leveraging DeepFace, a deep learning model for facial recognition, we ensure an immersive experience that resonates with your mood.

Emotion Detection Technology

How Does It Detect Your Emotions?

Our emotion detection system uses DeepFace, an advanced AI model that analyzes your facial expressions in real-time. Using facial landmarks—such as the curvature of your mouth, the position of your eyes, and other subtle markers—the system can determine if you're happy, sad, angry, surprised, or neutral.

Once your emotional state is detected, our system instantly generates personalized song recommendations to enhance or shift your mood. Whether you’re in need of a pick-me-up or a calming melody, the music provided will complement your emotions perfectly.

Why Music Affects Our Emotions

Music is an incredibly powerful tool for influencing emotions. Scientific studies have shown that our brains respond to musical stimuli by triggering emotions, memories, and even physical reactions. Fast-paced songs can boost energy levels, while slow and soothing melodies can calm the mind.

Our platform takes advantage of these emotional connections by offering a library of songs that align with the mood detected through facial recognition. Whether you need to relax after a stressful day or find joy in an uplifting beat, the music recommendations will match your emotional needs.
